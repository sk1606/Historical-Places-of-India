Discover Interesting Facts about Places

Taj Mahal, Agra

The Taj Mahal is not just a symbol of eternal love but also a UNESCO World Heritage Site, attracting millions of visitors every year.

Ajanta Caves, Maharashtra

The Ajanta Caves are renowned for their beautiful frescoes and carvings, representing Buddhist art and architecture dating back to the 2nd century BCE.

Golden Temple, Amritsar

The Golden Temple, also known as Harmandir Sahib, is the holiest shrine in Sikhism and is famous for its stunning golden structure and serene surroundings.

Amaravathi, Andhra Pradesh

Amaravathi is known for its ancient Buddhist heritage, housing one of the largest Buddhist stupas and being an important pilgrimage site for Buddhists.
