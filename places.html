Featured Historical Places

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is an architectural marvel built by Emperor Shah Jahan.

Ajanta Caves

The Ajanta Caves are famous for their rock-cut Buddhist monuments and ancient frescoes.

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, located in Amritsar, is the holiest site of Sikhism with its serene surroundings.

Amaravathi

Amaravathi is home to the ancient Amaravathi Stupa, a significant site in Indian Buddhism.
