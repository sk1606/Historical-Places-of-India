    • Taj Mahal, Agra

    Cultural Significance: The Taj Mahal symbolizes eternal love, combining Persian, Islamic, and Indian architectural styles.

    Famous Food: Agra Petha, Mughlai cuisine, especially kebabs and biryani.

    Ajanta Caves, Maharashtra

    Cultural Significance: Ajanta Caves are ancient Buddhist rock-cut monuments with intricate frescoes.

    Famous Food: Vada Pav, Misal Pav, and Puran Poli.

    Golden Temple, Amritsar

    Cultural Significance: The Golden Temple stands for equality, humility, and selfless service. The Langar serves free meals to thousands daily.

    Famous Food: Amritsari Kulcha, Makki di Roti with Sarson da Saag, and Lassi.

    Amaravati, Andhra Pradesh

    Cultural Significance: Amaravati was a significant Buddhist center, home to one of India's largest stupas.

    Famous Food: Pulihora, Gongura Pachadi, and Pootharekulu.
